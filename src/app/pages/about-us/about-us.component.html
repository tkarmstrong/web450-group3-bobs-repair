Our Story

My name is Bob Jr. and this company is my way of thanking my father, Bob for all this hard work and passion.
He was an understanding, down to earth, beer drinking guy with the longest patience and the biggest heart. When I was a young boy, my father and I would play play Pac-Man more times then I could count. I think I could play before I could even walk. We even had a special nickname for when we got in the zone, Blinky time. My father and I would discuss work, school, girls, Mom and everything in-between.
As I got older, so did my father and eventually we started getting into his favorite hobby, computer repair. He would fix, repair and even built computers for his friends and family. I remember asking my father why he didn’t want to do this as a job but he would always respond with he same thing, “I enjoy my hobby so why would i ruin it by making it work.” We continued to talk about everything but now it included college, marriage and lots more adult things. Over those years I grew to enjoy building, breaking, and tinkering with all sorts of electrons. My father and I would still find time to play Pac-Man though especially after a rough day.
After my graduation from college, my father suddenly passed away. My family and I were devastated and had a difficult time recovering. At this point, I was somewhat lost so knowing what my father would do, I started playing Pac-Man.
Years later, I had my first customer at my new computer repair shop. I named my shop after my father because he was the reason I got into this field. He was and is the inspiration be hide this place. I do think he had one thing wrong. If you love what you do you'll never work a day in your life.

Thank you Dad
